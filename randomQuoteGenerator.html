My Favorite places in Colorado

  • Aspen
  • Telluride
  • RockyMountainNationalPark
  • Aspen: the middle part of Colorado's fall:Fall is unforgettable at Aspen Snowmass. AspenFallDays can be warm and deceptively summer-like, followed by a sudden dusting of snow overnight. The abrupt temperature changes can set off a tidal wave of color among the aspen trees, willows and tundra plants, creating one of the most colorful displays of autumn in North America.
  • Telluride: TellurideFallthe south part of Colorado's fall:Typically, Fall colors start in mid to late September and last for up to three weeks. A memorable way to see the fall foliage is on a guided 4x4 tour to Tomboy Basin and Imogene Pass. The tour heads into Telluride's high country, where you'll travel above the treeline for spectacular views of the entire valley.
  • RockyMountainNationalPark: RockyMountainNationalParkthe middle part of Colorado's fall: Visiting Rocky Mountain National Park during the fall is a must do. Starting in late August, aspens in the highest reaches of the park begin turning a golden-yellow hue that’s made even prettier when they “quake” in the wind.
